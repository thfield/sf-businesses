About

The San Francisco Open Data Portal hosts a dataset of businesses that pay taxes to the city. This page queries that listing and allows you to search for businesses by their business license code.

Choose a license code from the list then click the "get businesses" button. The default behavior is to search for active business with any of the selected license codes. By choosing the "AND" radio button instead of "OR", the search will return active businesses that have all of the selected licenses.

The table of supervisor district shows the sum of found businesses within each San Francisco Supervisor District.
